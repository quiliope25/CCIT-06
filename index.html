Clash of Clans

In Clash of Clans, players claim a village and raise an army of Barbarians, War Wizards, Dragons and other mighty fighters. Players can then join a clan with others playing the game around the world to rise through the ranks together MMO-style, or create their own clans to contest ownership of the Realm. Players playing together as clan mates will be able to gather and strategize in real time through the game’s global chat function, as well as instantly donate troops to each other and trade resources in order to strengthen their defenses.
